# Practical Information

## Weather — Late May (climatology)

- **Temperature:** Max ~15°C / min ~9°C (Jersey Airport averages)
- **Rain:** ~54mm across ~9 days in May; cliff paths can be breezy (~11kn average wind)
- **Sunshine:** ~236 hrs in May — much sunnier than mainland UK
- **What to pack:** Layers, light waterproof, sun protection, walking shoes (cliff path), swimwear (hotel spa). Sea still cool
- **Day-of:** Specific 5–10 day forecast won't be reliable until 17–20 May 2026 — re-pull jerseymet.gov.je or Met Office close to departure

## Money & Payments

- **Currency:** Pound sterling (GBP). Jersey notes circulate alongside Bank of England notes — both accepted across the island
- **Cards:** Accepted at hotels, restaurants, attractions. Contactless ubiquitous
- **Cash:** Worth carrying a small float (~£40) for the BBQ at Faulkner Fisheries and harbour-stall purchases. Most ATMs free at point of use

## Connectivity

- **Mobile:** Most UK contracts cover Jersey at no extra cost (Channel Islands roaming is "Inclusive Roaming" on EE/O2/Vodafone/Three) — confirm with your provider before travel. Otherwise expect roaming charges
- **WiFi:** Hotel + most restaurants/cafes

## Getting Around

- **Hire car** is the assumed mode for this trip — public transport too slow for the full hub-and-spoke pattern
- **Driving on the left**, like the UK
- **Speed limit:** Island-wide max 40 mph
- **Green Lanes (15 mph):** marked at start/end with green-lane signage; pedestrians + cyclists have **priority**; common in inland St Lawrence, Trinity, St John, St Mary. Drive slow, expect to stop for walkers/cyclists, no aggressive overtaking
- **Other Jersey driving quirks:**
  - Traffic lights go **red → green direct, no amber**
  - **"Filter in Turn"** at marked junctions (give-way alternates)
  - **Yellow line across road = give way**
  - **Yellow line along kerb = no parking 24h**
  - **Phone-mount required** (no holding)
  - **UK licence valid** up to 12 months; keep left, overtake on right

## Parking

1. **Corbière** — small free car park at end of access road (busy at sunset; paid overflow at Corbière Phare housing area)
2. **Devil's Hole** — free at The Priory Inn
3. **Plémont** — small paid car park, then steep steps to beach
4. **Grève de Lecq** — paycard Mon–Sat, **free Sun + bank holidays**
5. **St Aubin** — paycard car parks along harbour Mon–Sat, **free Sun + bank holidays**
6. **Gorey Pier** (Sun 24) — **free Sundays**
7. **St Helier multi-storeys** — **free Mon 25 (bank holiday)**
