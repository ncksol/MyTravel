# Anchor Sights

The trip's anchor attractions, verified against official sources on 5 May 2026. Hours, prices, and access caveats below — re-confirm tide-dependent items the morning of.

---

### Mont Orgueil Castle

- **Where:** Gorey, St Martin (~25 min drive from St Helier)
- **Hours:** Daily 10:00–17:30 year-round
- **Price (2026):** Adult £19.50 / Senior £18.50 / Child £11.45
- **What:** 800-year medieval fortress, ~200 steps, views to the French coast. Café open
- **Live during our trip:** *Summer Night Sessions* (live music) start Fri 22 May; *Medieval Revels* runs 25–28 May 2026 (covers our Mon airport-day morning if we re-jig)
- **Day:** Sunday 24 May, 10:30–13:30
- **Source:** jerseyheritage.org

### Jersey War Tunnels

- **Where:** St Lawrence (~15 min drive from St Helier)
- **Hours:** Daily 10:00–17:00, last admission 16:00
- **Price (2026):** Adult £21 / Senior £20 / Child £10
- **What:** 1,000m+ underground complex — occupation-history exhibition, escape rooms, Liberation café, war-walk garden. Allow 2–3 hrs
- **Quietest:** 14:00–17:00
- **Day:** Saturday 23 May, 10:30–13:00
- **Source:** jerseywartunnels.com

### Corbière Lighthouse

- **Where:** St Brelade, SW tip (~25 min drive from St Helier)
- **Access:** Tidal causeway ~300m, ~10 min walk. **Cross only when Ports of Jersey causeway calculator confirms it's open** — re-check on the day. Warning siren sounds before tide covers
- **Best alignment in our window:** Sun 24 May (low water 20:17, sunset 20:56) or Sat 23 May evening (low 19:08)
- **Day:** Saturday 23 May, ~18:50–19:45 (walking out in good light, no sunset wait)
- **Sources:** ports.je coastguard, gov.je sunset, uktides.com

### Plémont Bay & cliff path

- **Where:** North coast (~15 min drive from L'Étacq)
- **Cliff section:** Plémont → Grève de Lecq out-and-back. ~1 hr each way. Wide sea views, headlands, wildflowers — most dramatic short slice of the north-coast path
- **Bay access:** Steep ~200 steps to the beach. Caves, waterfalls, rock pools open at low water
- **Parking:** Small paid car park at Plémont. **Free on bank holiday Mon 25 May**
- **Day:** Saturday 23 May, ~15:00–18:30

### Elizabeth Castle (skipped this trip)

- **Where:** St Aubin's Bay, opposite Westpark Slip (~10–12 min walk west of Grand Jersey)
- **Hours:** 10:00–17:30 daily through 24 Oct 2026; **last ferry from castle 17:00**
- **Price:** Castle + return ferry £24.50 adult / £23.50 senior; ferry-only £5
- **Access:** Amphibious "Castle Ferry" runs all day while castle is open, regardless of tide (weather-dependent). Kiosk at Westpark Slip
- **Tide caveat (this trip):** Sat 23 low 06:44/19:08 — outside opening; Sun 24 low 07:50 — before 10am opening; **Mon 25 low 08:58 means causeway safe ~07:30–10:30** — the only realistic walk-out window of the trip, but doesn't survive the 10:35 flight
- **Saved for:** future visit
- **Source:** jerseyheritage.org

### La Hougue Bie (skipped this trip)

- **Where:** Grouville (~15 min drive from St Helier)
- **Hours:** Open all 7 days through season including Sat 23 / Sun 24 / Mon 25 May 2026. Free volunteer tours daily 11:00. Tearoom 09:00–16:30
- **Price (2026):** Adult £15.45 / Senior £14.45 / Child/Student £10.25
- **Access caveat:** Neolithic chamber accessible via narrow ~10m duck-walk passage (mobility caveat)
- **Phone:** 01534 853823 to confirm specific hours
- **Saved for:** future visit
- **Source:** jerseyheritage.org

## Optional north-coast alternative

### Grève de Lecq → Devil's Hole walk

- **Distance:** One-way ~5.5–6 km, ~1.5–2 hrs at moderate pace; undulating cliffs, well-marked
- **Context:** Slice of Visit Jersey's official "North-West Coast" section (full route 12.2 km / 3hr 05 / 426m elevation, rated Challenging)
- **Trailhead parking:** Grève de Lecq public car park behind beach (paycard Mon–Sat 8–5; **free Sun + bank holiday**); free car park at Devil's Hole next to The Priory Inn pub
- **Eat/drink:** Le Moulin de Lecq (historic watermill pub, real ales, homemade food) or The Ugly Oyster Seafood Bar at Grève de Lecq end; Priory Inn at Devil's Hole end
- **Use as:** swap for Plémont→Grève de Lecq if conditions favour or you want a longer one-way with a pub at the end
